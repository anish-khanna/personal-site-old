Stock Market Performance Predictor

I've always been interested in stocks and how the economy works. I would hear stories about Wall Street Investors making loads of money just by predicting the movement of a stock. I don't have that kind of know-how or balls really, but I thought what better idea than to build an app that would connect my interest in stocks with something I do know a bit about, Machine Learning.

Play around with the chart and maybe even make your next big trade with its help. You never know, the machine might know a bit more about trading than you do.
